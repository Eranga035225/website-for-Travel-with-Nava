4) Jungle Beach, Unawatuna, Sri Lanka

Location
Click here to view on Map
Introduction:
Jungle Beach is a picturesque bay surrounded by lush jungle, located northwest of Unawatuna in the Southern Province of Sri Lanka. It’s an ideal spot for sunbathing and snorkeling
Route:
Tuk-Tuk Ride: Take a tuk-tuk (a three-wheeler taxi) from Unawatuna to the Jungle Beach path. The tuk-tuk will drop you off at the starting point of the trail.
Jungle Beach Path: From the starting point, follow the Jungle Beach path. It’s a scenic walk through lush greenery and jungle. Be prepared for some uphill sections, as the path can be steep in places.
Enjoy the Beach: Once you reach Jungle Beach, you’ll be rewarded with a beautiful bay surrounded by jungle. Relax on the sandy beach, swim in the clear waters, and explore the reef for snorkeling.
Water:
water sources are not available in campsite, it’s recommended to bring your own drinking water or a water filter.
